# Role: Customer Success Data Analyst

## Summary

Northstar Analytics is hiring a Customer Success Data Analyst to help HR customers interpret workforce data, identify staffing risks, and turn product insights into clear recommendations.

## Responsibilities

- Analyze customer workforce datasets and identify trends.
- Prepare concise recommendations for HR leaders and account teams.
- Partner with product managers on analytics quality and customer feedback.
- Communicate trade-offs and uncertainty in non-technical language.

## Required Skills

- SQL and spreadsheet analysis.
- Clear written and verbal communication.
- Experience translating data into customer-facing recommendations.
- Comfort handling sensitive people-related data.

## Success Signals

Strong candidates connect analytics work to customer outcomes, show privacy awareness, and explain how they build trust with non-technical stakeholders.
