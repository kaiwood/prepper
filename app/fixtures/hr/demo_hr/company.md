# Northstar Analytics

Northstar Analytics builds workforce-planning software for mid-market healthcare and logistics teams. The product helps HR leaders forecast staffing gaps, compare hiring scenarios, and explain workforce decisions with evidence.

## Mission

Make workforce planning clearer, fairer, and faster for teams that cannot afford staffing surprises.

## Values

- Evidence-led decisions over intuition-only planning.
- Customer empathy for HR teams, managers, and candidates.
- Privacy-first handling of employee and candidate data.
- Practical automation that keeps humans accountable.

## Interview-Relevant Facts

- Customers care about trustworthy explanations, not only dashboards.
- The company is expanding its customer success analytics team.
- Candidates should show interest in responsible AI, data quality, and measurable customer outcomes.
